[vee] now available for use at FreeShell.org

--

The  good  folks  over  at  FreeShell.org have installed vee (via
NetBSD's pkgsrc) onto iceland.freeshell.org, for members who wish
to use it for a minimalistic blogging experience.

  Join FreeShell.org

Note  that  in order to get the webspace, one must validate their
account, but it is easy and well worth it.

Once the account is validated, one simply needs to login to  ice-
land.freeshell.org to get their web space set up.

  FAQ - Making one's webpage accessible

Notes:

* also put into your ~/.profile, 'umask 022', otherwise each time
  vee is used, you'll have to rerun 'mkhomepg -p' in your $HOME so
  that the permissions get set right again

I  have set up my user page on Freeshell with a vee blog - though
it is really just full of test posts :)

  http://perlfan.tx0.org

Enjoy, and thanks again, SDF!

Cheers..
